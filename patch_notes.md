# Anki-City Patch Notes

Welcome to the new version of Anki-City! Here is a summary of the major changes, cost-balancing updates, and new gameplay features implemented recently:

---

## 🪙 1. Balanced Building Upgrade Costs
- **Smooth Progression Costs:** Completely balanced and tuned the resource upgrade costs for all buildings (Houses, Town Hall, Tavern, Straw Mill, Sawmill, Quarry, Gold Mine, and Crafting Hut) to ensure leveling up your city feels rewarding and fair.

---

## 🛠️ 2. New Building: Crafting Hut & Daily Artisans
- **The Crafting Hut:** Added the Crafting Hut (up to Level 5). Each upgrade levels up one of your daily artisans. At Level 5, all artisans are guaranteed Level 2.
- **Daily Artisans:** Choose one of your daily artisans to help you during your studies:
  - **Builder:** Completes shifts to permanently upgrade a random building in your city. It chooses randomly between your Houses (granting +2 population at Level 1, +5 at Level 2) and your Resource buildings (granting +1% double-drop chance at Level 1, +2% at Level 2). Permanent bonuses are visible in the building sky popovers.
  - **Merchant:** Finds extra random resource drops during study.
  - **Haggler:** Secures upgrade discounts on a random building type.
  - **Alchemist:** Transmutes common resources into rarer ones (e.g. Straw into Stone or Gold).
- **Artisan Selection:** Choose your active artisan daily from a draw of unique options. If you have not chosen anyone before, you can choose from all 4 options.

---

## 📖 3. Pre-credited Study Reviews for Artisans
- **Carryover Progress:** If you complete card reviews today before choosing your daily artisan, those reviews count towards their shift target. If you've already completed enough reviews today to meet their target, their effect triggers immediately upon selection.

---

## 🔥 4. Consecutive Study Streak & Milestones
- **50-Review Daily Minimum:** A day now only counts towards your consecutive study streak if you complete **at least 50 reviews** on that day.
- **Milestone Indicators:** The HUD next to the flame icon displays your consecutive streak and today's current milestone based on your reviews today (e.g. `🔥 4d 50 Ankis`, `🔥 4d 100 Ankis`).
- **Real-Time Milestone Drops:** Cross study milestones today (`50`, `100`, `200`, and `500` reviews) to receive **1 random resource** instantly with a toast notification in the card reviewer.

---

## 🍻 5. Tavern Trade Hub Expansion
- **Reverse Trading:** Leveling up your Tavern unlocks advanced trade ratios. At Level 4, you can reverse trade rarer resources back into common ones:
  - Trade 1x Gold back into 3x Stone (Lossless).
  - Trade 1x Stone back into 2x Wood (Taxed).

---

## 👥 6. Scaling Population Milestones
- Reaching city population milestones (every 100 population) now awards scaling resource rewards instead of a static 5:
  - **100 Pop:** 5 resource drops
  - **200 Pop:** 6 resource drops
  - **500 Pop:** 9 resource drops

---

## 🎭 7. Flavorful House Skins
- Level 4 houses now support cosmetic skins which can be purchased using your resources:
  - **Colourful House**
  - **Observatory House**
  - **Cozy Cottage**
  - **Mansion**

---

## 🎨 8. Visual & Alignment Fixes
- **Artisan Shop Position:** Fixed visual alignment issues for the Crafting Hut (Levels 1-5) so the buildings sit flat on the street instead of floating in the air.
- **Clean Assets:** Removed white background borders and pixels on high-level assets to blend in seamlessly with the dark interface.
